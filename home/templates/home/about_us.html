About Us

Our Mission

At Little Wheekers Rescue, our mission is to provide a safe haven for abandoned and neglected guinea pigs. We believe in promoting responsible pet ownership through education, adoption, and community outreach.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Little Wheekers Rescue has grown from a small volunteer effort to a thriving organization, dedicated to rescuing and rehabilitating guinea pigs in need. Our team is passionate about animal welfare and works tirelessly to ensure each guinea pig finds a loving home.

Where Donations Go

Your generous donations make a significant impact on the lives of our guinea pigs. Here's how your contributions are utilized:

  • High-quality hay for a balanced diet.
  • Nutritional pellets and fresh vegetables.
  • Regular veterinary check-ups and emergency medical care.
  • Clean bedding and habitat supplies.
  • Education and outreach programs for responsible pet ownership.
